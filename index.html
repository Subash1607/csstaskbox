My Favourite Food

Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish, mainly popular in South Asia. It is made with rice, some type of meat (chicken, goat, lamb, beef, prawn, or fish)

Pulihora

Pulihora is a traditional rice dish from Andhra Cuisine, made with rice, tamarind, curry leaves, spices and ginger

Bellam Paramannam

It is a simple sweet rice dish cooked in milk and traditionally made during festivals such as Navratri, Varalakshmi Vratham.

Paratha

Paratha is an Indian flatbread that is a staple in many Indian homes served for breakfast. It is made with whole wheat flour aka atta.
